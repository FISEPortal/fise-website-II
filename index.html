The Problem

You don't own
yourself online.

Every app, every platform, every AI tool you use was built on infrastructure that, by design, keeps your identity, your health data, your behavioral patterns, and your financial value locked inside someone else's system. That's not a bug. It's the business model.

AI, BCI, and Neurotech are generating the most sensitive data humanity has ever produced: the electrical activity of your mind, the biometric signature of your body, the neural patterns that reveal what you think and feel, before you've consciously decided any of it.

A 2024 Neurorights Foundation audit of 30 consumer neurotechnology companies found that 97% can and may transfer brain data to third parties, with most providing no meaningful limitations on that access.

And you cannot safely or ethically build the next generation of AI and Neurotech on a foundation built to extract rather than elevate.

"Safety and ethics are only as strong as the infrastructure beneath them. Policy without protocol is aspiration without proof."

The scientific community identified this gap in 2016 and has worked to address it at the policy layer. The protocol-layer infrastructure to make those protections enforceable has remained unbuilt, until now. Read our position paper: Beyond FAIR — The Missing Human Sovereignty Layer →

Primary Use Case

The most compelling frontier we've found: neural and biometric data.

Brain-Computer Interfaces

BCI devices generate continuous neural data, thought patterns, cognitive states, motor intentions. Today, manufacturers claim that data as their own. With FISE, you can assert ownership, monetize on your terms, and create a verifiable record that makes misuse documentable and actionable.

Neurotech & Wearables

From EEG headbands to biosensors, the consumer Neurotech market is expanding rapidly, with virtually no data governance beneath it. FISE is building the protocol layer designed to give Neurotech companies a path to compliant operation, and humans a foundation for safe participation.

Biotech & Health Data

Genomic data, biometrics, and health records are among the most valuable, and most exploited, data classes in existence. FISE's sovereignty architecture extends to biotech and is designed to give pharmaceutical research, clinical trials, and health data markets verifiable human consent and documented, transparent terms.

Agentic AI Systems

When AI agents act on someone's behalf, the question of authorization becomes a real liability issue: who approved this action, and can you prove it? FISE addresses this by anchoring each agent to a human-controlled decentralized identifier (DID), creating a traceable link between agent actions and the person who granted permission.
